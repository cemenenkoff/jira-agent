### 6.1
Personal devices are permitted for corporate email, calendar, and Teams only. They must be enrolled in Microsoft Intune for MDM management.

### 6.2
Enrollment establishes a managed work container. IT can remote-wipe only the corporate container; personal data is not touched.

### 6.3
Restricted and Confidential data must never be stored on a BYOD device outside the managed container.

### 6.4
Jailbroken or rooted devices are blocked from enrollment and from accessing corporate resources.

### 6.5
Operating systems must be within two major versions of current vendor releases. Older OS versions lose access automatically.

### 6.6
A $50/month BYOD stipend is available for roles flagged 'mobile-eligible' in Workday. Employees on the stipend forfeit eligibility for a corporate-issued mobile phone.
